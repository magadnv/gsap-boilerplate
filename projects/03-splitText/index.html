How we spend our days
is how we spend our lives.

Every scroll, every click, every quiet minute adds up. There's no separate "someday" where the real living starts — this is already it. The days aren't a rehearsal for the life; they are the life, one small choice at a time.

So the question isn't what you plan to do eventually. It's what today looked like. What you built. What you noticed. What you let slide by unattended.
